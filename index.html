Core Features

One App. Every Problem Solved.

StyleSync tackles all five pain points in the Davao barbershop experience — from the moment you decide you need a haircut to the moment you sit down in the chair.

AR Haircut Try-On — Style Lab

Point your camera at your face. Google ML Kit detects your face shape and overlays 17+ men's hairstyles in real-time using ARCore and ARKit — like a TikTok filter built for actual barbershop use. Send your chosen style directly to your barber before arriving.

Customer Feature 3 free previews ★ Unlimited with Premium

Live Queue Tracking — Queue HUD

Join any barbershop queue from home — no walking in, no calling. See your real-time position and estimated wait. Get push notifications when 1 person is ahead of you. Premium customers receive automatic priority placement.

Customer Feature ★ Priority placement with Premium

Smart Barber Booking

Choose your specific barber, pick a date and time slot, attach your AR style preview. The app matches you to the barber whose specialty best fits your chosen style. Payment is made in-person after the cut — simple and trusted.

Customer Feature In-person payment

Barber Dashboard & Queue Manager

Live queue management with drag-to-reorder controls. See each customer's chosen AR hairstyle before they arrive. Track daily earnings. Manage your portfolio of work to attract new clients across the Davao Region.

Barber Feature ₱99 Profile Boost available

Suki Loyalty System

After your 3rd booking with the same barber, both of you earn Suki Status — the traditional Filipino loyalty bond, digitized. Barbers identify their Power Clients. Customers are recognized for their loyalty. The Suki relationship is no longer just in memory.

Customer + Barber Auto-triggered at booking #3

SHA-256 Security + Barber Verification

All accounts are protected with SHA-256 password hashing, a unique cryptographic salt per user, and a server-side pepper — never stored in the database. Every barber is ID-verified by our admin team before receiving dashboard access.

All Users ID-verified barbers only
How It Works

From Queue to Chair in 3 Steps

StyleSync removes every friction point between deciding you need a haircut and sitting down in the barber's chair.

01

Preview Your Style

Open the AR Style Lab. The camera detects your face shape. Choose from 17+ men's hairstyles overlaid in real-time on your actual face. Save your preview to send to your barber.

02

Join & Track Your Queue

Find barbershops near you in Davao Region. See live wait times. Join the queue remotely from home. Get notified when 1 person is ahead of you so you know when to leave.

03

Show Up & Get Cut

Walk in at the right time. Your barber already sees your AR style preview. Get the exact cut you previewed. Pay the barber in-person after the haircut — cash or their personal GCash.

Payment is always in-person at the barbershop. StyleSync handles the queue and booking — the actual payment between you and your barber stays between you and your barber. Cash or the barber's own GCash after the haircut.
